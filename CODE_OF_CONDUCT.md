# Code of Conduct

VueGridle should be a respectful and practical place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, but be clear when something needs to change.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

Report concerns privately to `artemdoroshko555@gmail.com`.
